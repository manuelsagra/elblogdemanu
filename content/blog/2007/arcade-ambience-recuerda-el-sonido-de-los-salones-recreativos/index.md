Realmente curioso [este proyecto de Andy Hofle](http://arcade.hofle.com/), que pretende simular el barullo de un salón recreativo a lo largo de los años. Podremos oir los efectos de sonido de los juegos, las monedas cayendo al cajetín, la gente comentando las mejores jugadas, etc. Algo que viene de perlas si tenemos una "[maca](http://www.marcianitos.org/turecreativa.php)" en casa, o si queremos ponernos en situación durante una partidilla al [MAME](http://mamedev.org/).

La verdad es que cada una de las pistas tiene un curro tremendo, y en cada una se pueden escuchar los juegos típicos de cada época:

*   **1981**: "_Asteroids_", "_Astroblaster_", "_Berzerk_", "_Centipede_", "_Crazy Climber_", "_Crushroller_", "_Defender_", "_Donkey Kong_", "_Frogger_", "_Galaga_", "_Galaxian_", "_Gorf_", "_Missile Command_", "_Moon Cresta_", "_Mousetrap_", "_Ms. Pacman_", "_Omegarace_", "_Pacman_", "_Phoenix_", "_Qix_", "_Rally X_", "_Scramble_", "_Space Invaders_", "_Spectar_", "_Tempest_", "_Venture_", y "_Wizard Of War_".
*   **1983**: "_Bagman_", "_Bump n Jump_", "_Burgertime_", "_Congo Bongo_", "_Dig Dug_", "_Donkey Kong 3_", "_Donkey Kong Jr._", "_Elevator Action_", "_Food Fight_", "_Galaga_", "_Gyruss_", "_Joust_", "_Jungle Hunt_", "_Kangaroo_", "_Mappy_", "_Mario Bros._", "_Millipede_", "_Moon Patrol_", "_Mr. Do_", "_Ms Pacman_", "_Pengo_", "_Pleiads_", "_Pole Position_", "_QBert_", "_Robotron_", "_Sinistar_", "_Stargate_", "_Star Trek_", "_Tapper_", "_Time Pilot_", "_Track & Field_", "_Tron_", "_Xevious_", "_Zaxxon_", y "_Zoo Keeper_".
*   **1986**: "_Arkanoid_", "_Bank Panic_", "_Bubble Bobble_", "_Commando_", "_Excite Bike_", "_Flicky_", "_Galaga 3 (Gaplus)_", "_Galaga_", "_Gauntlet_", "_Ghosts N Goblins_", "_Gunsmoke_", "_Hat Trick_", "_Hoogan's Alley_", "_Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom_", "_Jail Break_", "_Kid Niki_", "_Mag Max_", "_Marble Madness_", "_Ms Pacman_", "_Pacland_", "_Paperboy_", "_Punch Out_", "_Rampage_", "_Road Fighter_", "_Rush'n Attack_", "_Sky Kid_", "_Spelunker_", "_Super Sprint_", "_Star Wars_", "_Tiger Heli_", "_Time Pilot 84_", y "_Two Tigers_".
*   **1992**: "_Altered Beast_", "_Arkanoid_", "_Cyberball 2072_", "_Galaga_", "_Golden Axe_", "_Klax_", "_Magician Lord_", "_Baseball Stars Professional_", "_Nam 1975_", "_Mortal Kombat_", "_Raiden_", "_Roadblasters_", "_Street Fighter 2_", "_Smash TV_", "_Tetris_", "_TMNT_", "_Toobin_", y "_Xybots_".

Los ficheros se pueden descargar independientemente, pero lo recomendable es utilizar [el tracker de PleasureDome](http://full.pleasuredome.org.uk/) (registro gratuito), para bajar todo de golpe, y de paso compartirlo con los demás. En cualquier caso, y a modo de pequeña donación, también se pueden comprar CDs con el audio sin comprimir directamente, por un precio simbólico.
